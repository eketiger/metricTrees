---
title: GDPR & privacy
description: Your rights under GDPR and how Metrics complies.
---

- **Export your data**: `GET /api/user/data-export` returns all data for your account.
- **Delete your account**: `DELETE /api/user/account` permanently removes your data.
- Analytics are opt-in and only load after you accept the consent banner.
- PII is never logged in plain text.
